Our Mission

At AutoMart, our mission is to create India's most trusted platform for buying and selling pre-owned vehicles. We believe every Indian deserves access to quality, affordable transportation.

Founded in 2018, we started with a simple idea: remove the information asymmetry and mistrust that plagued the used car market. We built technology that empowers both buyers and sellers with transparency, verified data, and fair pricing.

Today, with over 50,000 listings across 200+ cities, we're proud to be the go-to destination for second-hand car transactions in India.
