Absolutely. We strongly encourage test drives. Use the "Contact Seller" button on any car listing to arrange a test drive directly with the seller. Always meet in a safe, public location.
What documents should I check before buying a used car?+
Always verify: RC (Registration Certificate), Insurance papers (check validity), PUC certificate, Road Tax receipts, Service history, NOC (if from another state), Form 28/29/30 for ownership transfer.

How do I transfer car ownership safely?+
After agreeing on a price: 1) Sign Form 29 (Notice of Transfer) and Form 30 (Transfer of Ownership). 2) Buyer applies at the RTO for RC transfer. 3) Inform your insurer. 4) Keep copies of all forms. Always complete transfer within 30 days of sale.
