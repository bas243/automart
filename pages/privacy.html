Last updated: May 2025 · This policy applies to all users of AutoMart.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create a listing, contact a seller, or use our services. This includes:

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Car listing details you provide (brand, model, year, price, photos)
  • Messages and enquiries sent through our platform
  • Device information and browsing data via cookies

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve our platform
  • Connect buyers with sellers
  • Send you transactional notifications and support responses
  • Prevent fraud and ensure platform security
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information:

  • With other users as part of a listing or enquiry (e.g. your name and phone to a car seller)
  • With service providers who assist in our operations (e.g. Firebase by Google for database hosting)
  • When required by law or to protect our legal rights

4. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences, analyse site traffic, and improve user experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work as expected.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted database connections (Firebase), HTTPS for all data transmission, and access controls. However, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You can request deletion of your data by contacting us at support@automart.in.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at support@automart.in.

8. Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their policies before sharing any personal data.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of AutoMart after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
